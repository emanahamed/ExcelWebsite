Why Choose Excel Tutors

Ofsted Registered Tuition Centre

Rest assured, Excel Tutors is an Ofsted registered centre, ensuring a secure and supportive environment for all students. This accreditation affirms our commitment to high-quality, safe educational practices.

Bespoke Curriculum

Our curriculum is meticulously planned to cover all necessary material for exams, fully aligned with the National Curriculum and relevant exam boards. This approach ensures that every student is well-prepared and confident for their assessments.

Individual Attention

At Excel Tutors, we maintain small group classes averaging 4-5 students per tutor, ensuring personalized attention. Our classroom-based learning fosters a more intimate educational experience, far from the distractions of digital-only formats.

Regular Homework and Progress Exams

Homework is provided weekly to reinforce key concepts learned in class. Additionally, every six weeks, students undergo progress exams after a revision period. These exams are crucial in assessing their understanding and overall progress, helping both tutors and parents track academic development effectively.

Parent Communication

We believe in strong communication with parents. Through annual parents' evenings and quarterly reports, you are kept informed about your child's progress, understanding their achievements and areas for improvement in real-time.

Personal, Social, Emotional Development

We nurture not just academic skills but also personal growth. Students develop confidence, self-esteem, and emotional intelligence, learning to manage their feelings and build effective relationships within our supportive group settings.

Quality Checked Tutors

Our tutors are not only experienced professionals but are also rigorously vetted with DBS checks and reference checks. Their continuous training ensures they remain at the forefront of educational methods and knowledge.

Modern Facilities

Our modern facilities include a dedicated computer suite, ensuring students have access to the latest technological tools and resources. This helps keep our teaching methods up-to-date and relevant, providing students with a comprehensive learning experience.
