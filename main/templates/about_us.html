Excel Tutors: Our Story

Excel Tutors

Background Story

Founded in 2008, Excel Tutors has supported thousands of young learners in Tower Hamlets and its environs to achieve distinguished grades in their GCSEs and GCE A Level exams. The Proprietor started Excel Tutors with just 3 learners. Notwithstanding the challenging circumstances encountered by every new organisation, through determination and forthright attitude towards work, the centre grew in leaps and bounds and to date can boasts of over 800 young learners registered to receive tuition support in addition to the over 70 Tutors who are experts in their respective fields. Through hard work, focusing on the learning needs of young learners, the centre has grown significantly and become a household name within the Tower Hamlets community and its environs in East London.

of London and its neighbouring counties and boroughs to ensure that our tried and tested tailor

Mission

Our mission evolves from our visionary philosophy. We strive to:
  • Identify a child’s strengths and challenges upon initial and on-going assessment
  • Design bespoke learning support activities in relation to findings from initial assessments
  • Track learner progress over time, making judgments on individual learner progress
  • Revise teaching and learning strategy to enhance learner performance
  • Focus on individual specific challenges
  • Develop learners’ critical thinking and independent learning abilities
  • Support all learners’ to develop a positive attitude towards their studies
